About me

I am a lecturer and researcher specialising in machine learning for computer networks. My doctoral dissertation focuses on a Adaptive Bandwidth Control Based on Machine Learning.

I serve as Managing Editor of Jurnal RESTI (Scopus (Q3), Sinta 2), and as a reviewer for Elsevier journals including Computer Networks (Q1) and Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence (Q1). I am also an active board member of IAII Nusantara and Badan Kejuruan Informatika – PII.
